Client Name: Emily Shaffer (New Customer) - Final Decision Maker
Business Name: Tattered Meadows
New or Redesign New Website
Website Type: Marketing, Community, Shopping, Blogging
Client Goal: Clients goal is to have a platform to blog about her creative process behind her clothes upcycling. She also wishes to be able to provide users the ability to buy her available pieces directly on the site.
Audience Issues, Concerns, and Needs Approach
Shoppers:
People that want to buy a piece of clothing after visiting her instagram. They enjoy handmade and upcycled clothing made from small business owners. Values supporting a local artists or business and is willing to pay more for the labor required.
  • Built-in Shopping ability
  • Store Inventory
  • Returns
  • Sizing
  • Either build shopping feature from scratch or utilize some framework or service that provides shoping cart functionality and payment options.
  • Include page for browsing available piece for sale.
  • Build page with clearly defined return policy and instructions.
  • Include ability to sort by size
Hobbyists:
People who enjoy the process of how clothes are made or reused in different ways. They like to learn different techniques regarding sowing or working with different fabrics. They enjoy learning about someone elses approach to clothes making.
  • Index of topics
  • Pictures
  • Blog Search
  • Organize posts with topics and allow for filtering these results.
  • Incorporate pictures into blog posts that document the clients process.
  • Allow for users to search for particular keywords and show blog posts that contain them.
Agencies/Partners:
These are agencies/partners that may be interested in working with the client; other small businesses looking to collaborate or bigger brands wanting to partner.
  • Business Inquiry
  • Address
  • Implement a way to contact client specificly for business inquiries
  • Provide an address used for collaberations to send material to client
